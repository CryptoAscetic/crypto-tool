how to buy
make wallet
download phantom or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to phantom.io.
Get Some SOL
have SOL in your wallet to switch to $SIF. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
Go to Raydium
connect to Raydium. Go to app.Raydium.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $SIF token address into Raydium, select $SIF, and confirm. When phantom prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
Switch SOL for $SIF
